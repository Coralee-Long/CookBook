Raan (Mutton) Roast

Beginner

Dairy-Free

Low Carb

Healthy

One-Pot

45 Min

serves 8

|

prep 15min

|

cook 20min

|

ready 10min

This is a super easy and delicious Roast leg of lamb recipe or simply Raan roast AKA mutton leg roast. The recipe is simple, the trick in making raan roast is the soft melt in the mouth meat.

Ingredient Amount
mutton leg 1.5kg
lemon juice 4 tbsp
ginger paste 1 tbsp
onion, medium 1
yogurt 1.5 cups
green chilli paste 1 tbsp
ground cumin 4 tbsp
turmeric 1 tsp
salt 0.5 - 1 tsp
cardamom pods whole 15
cinnamon 0.5 tsp
garam masala 2 tsp
crushed tomatoes or diced tomatoes 14oz can
chicken pieces 1.5 lb
garlic finely chopped 2 tsp
brown sugar 2 tsp
lemon juice 1 tbsp
black pepper coarsely ground 2 - 3 tsp
Instructions
Step 1
In a pan roast round red chilli, coriander and cumin seed for 30 second until fragrant.
Step 2
Add red chilli powder, all spice powder and salt. Give it a stir and transfer to a food processor.
Step 3
Grind mixture into a fine powder. Pass the powder through a seive and grind the remains once again. Seive again. Then discard the remains.
Step 4
Mix spice mix, lemon juice, ginger, garlic and yogurt in a bowl. Set aside.
Step 5
Wash the mutton leg thoroughly, and leave the leg in colander to dry. Poke the leg all around with a fork, then apply spice mixture throughly all over the leg.
Step 6
Cover with cling wrap and store in fridge over night. (8-16 hours). Next day, cook the mutton leg in wide pot with 2 cups of water. Keep the heat on medium low and cover the pot tightly to seal the steam.
