Amanqina Enkukhu (Chicken Feet)

Beginner

Dairy-Free

Low Carb

Healthy

One-Pot

45 Min

serves 8

|

prep 15min

|

cook 20min

|

ready 10min

If you’re an adventurous eater and have a taste for unconventional food, then amanqina is right up your alley. Known colloquially as “walkie talkies” or “runaways”, it refers to a stew made with chicken feet. It’s a common township snack food popular throughout the country.

Ingredient Amount
chicken feet 1kg
chicken stock cube 1
rajah curry powder 1 tbsp
crushed chilli flakes 2 tbsp
chicken spice 1 tbsp
bbq spice 2 tsp
braai spice 1 tsp
powdered chicken soup 2 tbsp
black pepper coarsely ground 0.5 - 1 tsp
salt 1 tsp
Instructions
Step 1
Rinse the chicken feet cause most bought from butchers are clean so you will rinse and let them boil until they are soft almost falling off of the bone
Step 2
Add the chicken stock and pour your spices and stir
Step 3
Let them cook until the lower the heat and prepare the soup by mixing it with water and pour it over the chicken feet (amanqina) they are tasty enjoy
